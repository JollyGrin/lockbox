Getting Started

Join the first crowdfund!

You're about to experience the cutting edge combination of blockchain and IoT. Before you jump onto the NestEgg platform, be sure to equip yourself with an ethereum wallet and the door-unlocking app. You'll need both of them to participate and make reservations.

Step 1: Euro Tokens

Convert your euro into tokens!

To interact with smart-contracts, you need euro-tokens. You can aquire euro-tokens by paying with ether or a bank wire. Once you have euro-tokens in your wallet, you can join the crowdfund!


Get Euro Tokens

Step 2: Sign Up

Register on our platform

Jump over to the NestEgg platform and register an account. Be sure to double-check your profile information before continuing to the crowdfund.


Signup on NestEgg!

Step 3: Invest

Join the first crowdfund

Head over to "Browse New Eggs" and use your euro-tokens to invest in the project. Check if your Metamask is on the "Rinkeby" testnet.


Signup on NestEgg!


Make a Reservation

Finally! Time to use the lockbox!

Once the crowdfund is complete, reservation hours are issued as credits every week to the shared owners. You'll be using your dashboard to claim your credits and make reservations with it.
