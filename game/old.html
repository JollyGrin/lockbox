Getting Started
with the Lockbox!

The Lockbox is new sharing economy platform that runs on ethereum.
Let's walkthrough all the steps needed before we make our first reservation.

Pre-Requirements

Download your Blockchain & IoT apps

You're about to experience the cutting edge combination of blockchain and IoT. Before you jump onto the NestEgg platform, be sure to equip yourself with an ethereum wallet and the door-unlocking app. You'll need both of them to participate and make reservations.

Meta-Mask

An ethereum wallet

Installed as a browser plugin, you'll need this to make payments and sign messages.

Noke

Open bluetooth locks
